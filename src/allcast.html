Echelon: AllCast

A guide on the current and upcoming features of the app

80%

Purpose

AllCast is a very similar mode to the Jukebox, except with one key difference, all users will be able to play on their own devices. This mode is excellent for remote group music sessions. AllCast is great to take a jog or chill at home while listening to a list of tracks curated live by you and your friends.

Progress

Since AllCast is essentially the same functionality of Jukebox, it's progress is very dependent on the progress of Jukebox. There are only minor changes and adjustments to make in order to make AllCast functional.
